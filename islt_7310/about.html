I'd like to share with you some details about me and my background.
As an undergraduate, I pursued an interdisciplinary degree in Art, Art History and Anthropology. But life had other plans. To start a family, I stopped going to college and eventually started a career in the IT field. In my work, I've I gained some experience in Learning Technologies. I have been a technical trainer, teaching classes in the Adobe suite and Microsoft Office. I have also helped students with Canvas courses. When students have problems getting into or using their courses, I have helped them get access and get oriented in their courses. I have also designed course certificates in Canvas Catalog.

Experience isn't everything, but it helps.

Later, I decided to go back and finish my degree. After I finished, I realized my real passion was training and education and made the decision to get my Master’s degree in Learning Technologies.

In my private time, I enjoy music and art. My wife and I have three amazing kids. We share our lives with a small but mighty Jack Russell terrier.
